How does it work?

1. Investment VIP Selection

Dovekzy offers a variety of VIPs with a duration of 30 days, and the return rates vary depending on the price of the VIP purchased.

2. Buy or get VIPs

You can buy one or more VIPs according to your preference and each VIP has a profit percentage that depends on the price of the VIP. Also once it expires you can buy it again.

Important: If you buy a VIP, every 24 hours you can use the "CLAIM REWARD" button so don't forget it!

3. Balance withdrawals

You can withdraw your balance once it goes from $10 to your crypto wallet that accepts a network: Tron(TRC20)

4. Referrals

If you have referrals, you can automatically get 10% of the price of each VIP they buy. The number of referrals is unlimited!!!
